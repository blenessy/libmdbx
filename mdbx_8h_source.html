334  * argument.
335  *
336  * Otherwise, if MDBX was builded not as a DLL, some black magic
337  * may be required depending of Windows version:
338  * - Modern Windows versions, including Windows Vista and later, provides
339  * support for "TLS Directory" (e.g .CRT$XL[A-Z] sections in executable
